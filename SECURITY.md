## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of VIVA very seriously. If you have found a security vulnerability, please **DO NOT** open a public issue.

### How to Report

Please email us directly at [your-email@example.com] with the subject line `[SECURITY] VIVA Vulnerability`.

Include as much information as possible:
- Type of issue (e.g., SQL Injection, XSS, etc.)
- Steps to reproduce
- Affected components

We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours and provide a timeline for the fix.

### Bounty Program

We currently do not offer a paid bug bounty program, but we will happily list you in our Hall of Fame for responsible disclosure.
